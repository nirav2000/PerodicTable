Periodic Table Palace

Build your 00-99 number-image system, customise vivid element images, walk a full 118-locus memory palace, and follow a 7-day intensive plan.

How to link information

  1. Element image: Turn the element into a vivid scene.
  2. Atomic number image: Convert the number into your 00-99 cue.
  3. Collision: Make both images interact violently or absurdly.
  4. Locus: Drop the combined scene on the fixed palace location.
  5. Recall: Walk the palace and decode place → number → element.

Suggested daily use

  • 10 minutes: number-image review
  • 10 minutes: element encoding
  • 5 minutes: palace walkthrough
  • 5 minutes: random quiz or teach-back
